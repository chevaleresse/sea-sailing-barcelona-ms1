Frequently Asked Questions

Do you have any doubts or questions? Do you want to know anything related to sailing or booking with us? The asnwer may be on this page. If not, please, get in touch with us and we will get back to you as soon as possible.

On board, we have a wide variety of drinks and snacks. The drinks and snacks are included in the price. Drinks include water, cola, fanta, orange juice, beer, wine, cava, port, tea, coffee. Snacks include chips, nuts, salami, cheese, olives, fuet and crackers.
We are located in Port Vell. This is an exclusive private marina in the Barceloneta area. Our address is Moll De La Barceloneta, 1 OneOcean Port Vell, 08003 Barcelona Spain. You can easily access the harbour with a taxi, foot, or metro. With the metro, you can take line 4, yellow line, and exit at Barceloneta. From there, it is around 3 minutes walking to the entrance of the harbour.
Yourself! Depending on the weather, you can bring your swimsuit and towel with you. It is advised to bring sunglasses and sun cream with you. It can be windy on the sea, thus, a jacket is advised depending on the weather. For the check-in, you should bring your booking confirmation with you.
Yes, it is possible to access this activity with a wheelchair, however, it is challenging. Are you, or one of your group members, impaired, please let us know when you book and we will make arrangements to provide assistance.
You can book your Sailing Adventure by getting in touch with us! We would love to have you on board. Send us a WhatsApp message or email, or give us a call. You can also fill out our contact form and we will get back to you as soon as possible to arrange your Sailing Adventure.
It is possible to cancel your booking, with full reimbursement, 7 days before your adventure starts. If you wish to cancel your booking less than 7 days before the start of your adventure, 50% of the price will be reimbursed. If you wish to cancel within 24 hours of the start of the adventure, no reimbursement will be made.
Yes. If you wish to amend your sailing adventure 7 days before your adventure starts, you can change the day for free, depending on our availability. If you wish to amend your sailing adventure within 7 days of the initially set sailing day, the costs of amendment is €75. If you wish to amend the sailing adventure day and time within 24 hours of when your adventure was initially set to, the costs of amendment is €150.
Yes, it is allowed to bring your own drinks and you are allowed to consume alcohol on board. However, always drink responsible.
No, we do not allow smoking on board of our yachts.
Yes, if the weather allows for it. The skipper makes the decision in that moment to allow you to dive into the Mediterranean sea and take a refreshing swim.
If the weather is unfavourable to sail, the skipper will decide whether to go out or not. If the skipper decides not to, we will re-arrange your sailing adventure. If the adventure is cancelled on our hand, due to the weather, you can also opt for a reimbursement.
Yes, we do have a WIFI connection on board and have our own sound system. If you want, you can bring your own music and connect with our sound system. Also, it is possible charge your devices on board.
