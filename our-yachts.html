Lorena - Jeanneau SO49 performance

Lorena is a 49ft luxury sailing yacht with a wide variety of features, making your stay comfortable. This sailing yacht, a Jeanneau SO49 Performance yacht, was build in 2006 and has been refitted in 2015. It is a comfortable sailing yacht with 4 cabins and 3 toilets on board

Yacht specifications
  • 4 cabins
  • 3 toilets
  • 9 knots cruise speed
  • Safety line
  • Icebox in cockpit
  • Dinghy
  • Hot water
  • Deck shower
  • GPS
  • Autopilot
  • TV
  • WII
  • Kayak
  • Freezer
  • Gas cooker
  • Barbecue

BomBon - Bavaria Yachtbau 50 Cruiser

The BomBon is a classic 49ft sailing yacht, build in 2006. The sailing yacht has been refitted in 2013. This luxury sailing yacht has 12 day spaces and 5 cabins for overnight stay. On board are 3 toilets, liferafts, Battery Chargers, Hot water, Music and other features to provide an amazing experience on board. The BomBon has a maximum speed of 8 knots.

Yacht specifications
  • 5 cabins
  • 3 toilets
  • Liferafts
  • Battery Chargers
  • Hot water
  • Music installation
  • Dinghy
  • Autopilot
  • Snorkling equipment
  • Deck shower
