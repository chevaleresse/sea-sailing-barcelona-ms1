Sea Sailing Adventure Details

Live on a luxury yacht for several days. Experience life on sea and have the best time of your life. Sail on the Mediterranean sea, or, if your adventure lasts more than 5 hours, sail to an island and tour around it, discovering its hidden gems.Together, we will explore some of the hidden secrets and unknown destinations the Mediterranean has in store. Pricing starts from €299, per person. Adventures are for up to 12 people.

Book your Sailing Adventure now!

What is included in our Sea Sailing Adventure?

Join us on a one-day tour of up to 10 hours and sail with us on the Mediterranean sea and to one of the amazing islands. Depending on the duration of the adventure you can choose to sail on the sea or head to one of the islands to discover. You can sail around the island, or, set to land and discover the island itself, if the duration of the tour allows for it. You can also swim, snorkle or paddle,in the sea and along the coast, if the weather allows for it. This is definitely a reccomendation!

The price starts from €299 per person, for 2 hours. Get in touch with us to receive full pricing details on the adventure of your preference.

What is included in the adventure:

  • Sailing Yacht (max. 12 people)
  • Professional Skipper (with RYA skipper license)
  • Drinks and snacks
  • Music
  • Guided tour to hidden gems and secrets of the Mediterranean
  • Safety equipment (e.g. life jackets and first aid kit)
  • Fuel costs
  • Paddle board and snorkling equipment

What is not included:

  • Docking/harbour fees, if mooring at a port on an island
  • Lunch and dinner (this can be arranged against additional costs)
  • Flight pick-up and transfers

What to expect?

We will start our Sea Sailing Adventure in the private marina of the exlusive harbour Port Vell, in Barceloneta. First, we start with a safety briefing and get setteled for our stay on the yacht. Then we will head out for the sea. We will sail along the coast of Barcelona and then, head for the open sea. Our skipper will guide you guide you on this experience, and if you desire, the captain may even teach you a little how to sail.
