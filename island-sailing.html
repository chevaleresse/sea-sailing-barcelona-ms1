What to expect?

We will start our Island Sailing Adventure in the private marina of the exlusive harbour Port Vell, in Barceloneta. First, we start with a safety briefing and get setteled for our stay on the yacht. Then we will head out for the sea. We will sail along the coast of Barcelona and then, head for the open sea. We will sail to multiple islands depending on the duration of your adventure. A recommendation is Mallorca. Our skipper will guide you guide you on this experience, and if you desire, the captain may even teach you a little how to sail.

To book your Island Sailing Adventure, please get in touch with us. Please, share your preferred sailing day, starting time and duration (1-8 days), and we will arrange the tour to your needs.
